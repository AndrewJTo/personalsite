Covid19 Test Site - DHSC/ Mitie

When I graduated university it turned out that, due to global events, there were not as many opportunities for gainful employment as I was expecting. There were however, quite a few jobs going for testing people for this new virus, so I took a job at my local test site in Gloucester. The site was being run by the large PFI company Mitie for PHE the DHSC and its management really opened my eyes to the inside workings of a large Government contract.

The title for this role was 'Traffic Marshal' however it involved many things, including: Telling people where to drive, checking them into the site, making test kits, instructing people on them, and collecting the kits at the end. To begin with the work was quite rewarding as you knew you were making a difference, but towards the end I grew frustrated with telling people they could not have a test because of lab shortfalls.
